# Audistill Brand Kit

## Brand Overview

**Name:** Audistill
**Tagline:** Distill knowledge from every conversation.
**Category:** Local-first AI audio knowledge base for macOS

### Name Meaning

Audio + Distill — the act of refining raw audio into concentrated, retrievable knowledge. Like distilling spirits from grain, Audistill extracts the essence from spoken content.

### Brand Personality

**Warm Companion** — approachable, focused, trustworthy. Not cold or clinical. Audistill is the quiet assistant that remembers everything you heard so you don't have to.

**Voice traits:**
- Calm and confident, never loud
- Helpful without being pushy
- Smart without being academic
- Local and private — your knowledge stays yours

## Messaging Framework

### Elevator Pitch

Audistill is a macOS app that turns podcasts, meetings, and lectures into a searchable personal knowledge base — entirely on your device. Drop in audio, get structured summaries you can search, organize, and revisit.

### Value Propositions

| For | Pain Point | Audistill Solves |
|-----|-----------|-----------------|
| Knowledge workers | Consume hours of audio, retain nothing | Automatic structured summaries at multiple detail levels |
| Privacy-conscious users | Don't trust cloud transcription | 100% local transcription, on-device AI |
| Podcast learners | No way to search what you heard last month | Persistent searchable library with full-text search |
| Meeting attendees | Action items lost after the call ends | Comprehensive notes generated from any recording |

### Key Messages

1. **Local-first, always private.** Your audio never leaves your machine. Transcription runs entirely on Apple Silicon — no cloud, no subscriptions for the core pipeline.

2. **From audio to knowledge in minutes.** Drop a file, get a structured summary. Brief for scanning, detailed for reference, full for when you need everything.

3. **Your personal audio library.** Every podcast, meeting, and lecture — organized, searchable, and always available. Build knowledge over time, not just consume it.

4. **Smart summaries, your rules.** Three detail levels. Custom instructions. Language-aware. The AI adapts to your content — meetings get action items, lectures get key concepts.

### Tone of Voice (Marketing Copy)

- **Conversational but precise** — write like you're explaining to a smart friend
- **Benefits over features** — "never forget what you heard" not "uses ONNX runtime"
- **Confident without hype** — no "revolutionary" or "game-changing"
- **Grounded** — specific examples, real use cases, honest about what it is
- **Warm** — use "you/your" generously, acknowledge the reader's time
